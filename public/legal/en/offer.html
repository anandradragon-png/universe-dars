The legally binding offer is the Russian version: Публичная оферта (RU). This is a short English summary.

What you buy

Access to digital features inside YupDar: subscription tiers (Guardian, Master), the full Book of DARs, and one-time add-ons. All offerings are listed on the Plans page in the app.

Prices

Prices are shown in the Plans section in the app. For international users, payment goes through Telegram Stars or DarAI (NEAR Protocol). YooKassa is for Russian residents only.

How subscriptions work

You pay for a chosen period (1, 3, 6, or 12 months). Access opens immediately. No auto-renewal. When the period ends, you return to free tier — your data stays.

How one-time products work

The Book of DARs is a forever purchase. Add-ons (extra Oracle, compatibility PDF, parenting book chapter, Hero Journey unlocks) work as described next to each item.

Refunds

For Telegram Stars: contact Telegram support. For YooKassa: contact us via the in-app feedback form. For DarAI: crypto transactions are non-refundable.

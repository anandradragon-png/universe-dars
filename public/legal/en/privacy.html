YupDar — Privacy Policy

Short English version. The full, legally binding policy is in Russian: Политика конфиденциальности (RU).

What we collect

  • Your Telegram ID, name, and username — to identify you in the app
  • Date of birth, gender (optional) — to calculate your DAR
  • Names and dates of birth of people you add to your "Family" section
  • Your progress in features (Treasury, Hero Journey, Diary)
  • Payment metadata (provider, amount, date) — but not card numbers

What we do NOT collect

  • Phone number, email (unless you give it for receipt)
  • Location, contacts list
  • Card numbers or any payment details — all payments go through Telegram, DarAI, or YooKassa

Where data is stored

Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database, EU region — Ireland). Access protected by Row Level Security.

Third parties

We use: DeepSeek and Groq (for AI-generated text), Telegram Bot API (for payments and messages), Supabase (database). Each operates under their own privacy terms.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time. Contact us via the in-app feedback form. We process such requests within 30 days.

Cookies

The app uses minimal local storage to remember your language and session. No tracking cookies.

Contact

Privacy questions: support@yupland.com

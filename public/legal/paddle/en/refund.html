YupDar — Refund Policy (International)

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This policy applies to international payments processed through Paddle (our Merchant of Record). For payments made via YooKassa (Russian residents), separate Russian terms apply.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

You may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase, for any reason. No questions asked.

2. How to Request a Refund

Send a refund request to support@yupdar.com or via the in-app feedback form. Include:

  • Your order number or the email used at purchase
  • The date of your purchase
  • A brief reason (optional, but helps us improve)

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days back to the original payment method.

3. Subscription Renewals

If your subscription renewed automatically and you did not intend to renew, contact us within 7 days of the renewal charge for a full refund of that renewal period, provided you have not actively used the renewed period.

4. Partial Refunds for Long-Term Subscriptions

For 3, 6, or 12-month subscriptions, if you request a refund after the 14-day window:

  • We may issue a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of your subscription at our discretion
  • The pro-rated amount is calculated based on the standard monthly price (without long-term discount)

5. One-Time Purchases (Digital Goods)

For one-time purchases of digital products (such as the Book of DARs PDF or unlock of a single DAR profile):

  • Refunds are available within 14 days if the content has not been downloaded or accessed
  • Once a digital product has been accessed or downloaded, it is generally non-refundable, in accordance with EU and UK consumer protection law for digital content delivered immediately upon purchase

6. What's Not Refundable

  • Payments older than 14 days (except subscription renewals as described above)
  • Digital content that has been delivered and accessed (e.g., downloaded PDFs, unlocked DARs)
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Service fees or taxes already remitted to authorities by Paddle

7. Exceptional Cases

If you experienced a technical issue that prevented you from using YupDar, or if you were charged incorrectly, we will refund you in full regardless of the time elapsed. Please describe the issue clearly in your refund request.

8. Chargebacks

We kindly ask you to contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Most issues can be resolved quickly through direct communication, and chargebacks may result in account termination.

9. Currency and Fees

Refunds are issued in the same currency as the original payment. Any currency conversion losses or bank fees are not refunded by us.

10. Contact

For any refund-related questions:

  • Email: support@yupdar.com
  • In-app feedback form
  • Telegram: @YupDarBot

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

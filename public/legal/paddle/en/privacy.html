YupDar — Privacy Policy (International)

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This policy applies to international users paying through Paddle. Russian residents are subject to a separate Russian policy: Политика конфиденциальности (RU).

1. Who We Are

YupDar is operated by an individual entrepreneur registered in Georgia. We are the data controller for the information you provide through the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account data: Telegram ID, name, username
  • Profile data: date of birth, optional gender (used to calculate your DAR)
  • Family data: names and birth dates of people you add to your "Family" section
  • Feedback: any messages you send via the in-app feedback form
  • Email: only if provided for receipt, refund, or support

2.2 Information generated automatically

  • Usage data: features you access, progress in Treasury, Hero Journey, Diary, ARKA
  • Technical data: Telegram client version, language preference, IP-derived country (for tax purposes via Paddle)
  • Payment metadata: transaction ID, amount, currency, date (no card numbers)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Phone numbers (unless voluntarily shared for support)
  • Precise location, contacts list, photos, files outside the app
  • Card numbers or full payment details (handled entirely by Paddle)
  • Browsing history outside YupDar

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide the service you subscribed to
  • Legitimate interest: to improve the app and prevent abuse
  • Consent: for optional features (e.g., diary, family section)
  • Legal obligation: tax records, fraud prevention, KYC by payment providers

5. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver the YupDar service (calculating your DAR, generating personalized content)
  • To process payments through Paddle
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To improve the app (in aggregated/anonymized form)
  • To comply with legal obligations

6. Where Your Data Is Stored

Data is stored in Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL database hosted in the EU region (Ireland). Access is protected by Row Level Security — each user can only see their own data.

7. Third Parties We Share Data With

We share only the minimum necessary data with the following service providers, each operating under their own privacy terms:

  • Paddle.com Market Limited (UK) — Merchant of Record. Receives: email, country, billing data, transaction info. Paddle Privacy Policy
  • Telegram — message delivery, identity verification. Telegram Privacy
  • DeepSeek and Groq (AI providers) — receive anonymized prompts to generate personalized text. No personal identifiers are sent.
  • Supabase — secure database storage (EU/Ireland)
  • Vercel — hosting and content delivery

8. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to providers in the UK, US, and EU under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

9. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: limit how we process your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email support@yupdar.com. We respond within 30 days.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except for records we must keep for legal or tax compliance (typically up to 7 years for payment records, as required by Georgian and EU tax law).

11. Children

YupDar is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Cookies and Local Storage

The app uses minimal local storage to remember your language preference, session, and progress. No third-party tracking cookies, no advertising trackers.

13. Security

We use industry-standard security: HTTPS encryption, Row Level Security on the database, regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure, but we apply best practices to protect your data.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be announced inside the app at least 14 days before they take effect.

15. Contact and Complaints

  • Privacy questions: support@yupdar.com
  • EU users may also file complaints with their national data protection authority

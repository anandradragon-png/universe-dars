YupDar — Cookies & Local Storage

What we use

YupDar runs primarily as a Telegram Mini App. We use the browser's localStorage to remember:

  • Your chosen language (ru / en / es)
  • Your tariff cache (for fast UI)
  • Local progress hints (e.g. last opened DAR)

No tracking cookies

We do not use third-party analytics cookies, advertising trackers, or Google Analytics.

How to clear

You can clear local storage at any time via the "Full reset" button in your Personal Cabinet.
